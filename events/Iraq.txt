# PLAN FOR THE INSURGENCY MECHANIC

# Each side will have number of troops, GOVT will have readiness as well
# Readiness will increase anytime you conduct an attack, and readiness will also decrease if you fuck up attacks or get fucked in an attack by the enemy
# Readiness will determine how successful attacks are, more readiness = less troops killed + higher chance of success
# Attacking: 4 things could happen. 1. You succeed, get kills and take the state. 2. You attack, lose few people, but don't get territory.
# Attacking Continued: 3. You attack, but don't inflict much damage. 4. You done fucked up, and lose A LOT of troops.
# Attacking will cost Manpower, equipment, and money. Everytime you attack, there is a chance of radicalizing the populus, increasing Salafism.
# Defending: If you don't negotiate with Shi'ites, they will attack you. If you're being attacked, and have more than 40% readiness, you will just lose troops
# State modifiers will mark if there is an insurgency. Jihadists will give a negative modifier
# Shi'ites will also give a negative modifier, but if you negotiate with them then their negative modifier will go away
# If Jihadists, or Shi'ite forces have less than 20,000 troops, they will capitulate
# Each month, Jihadists will gain a certain amount of troops based on the popularity of Salafism
# Shi'ites and Kurds will gain troops, but it will be nominal at most (As it was in the real conflict)
# The Government forces can get troops at larger quantities, but the more troops they have, the more it will cost
# If the player is unable to defeat the insurgents and they get more than 100,000 troops, Al-Qaeda will declare a war and will receive the states it owns
# The player will also fight the Shi'ites if they have not negotiated with them once the civil war breaks out
#
